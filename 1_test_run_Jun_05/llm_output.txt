<h2>Prompt Tip of the Day</h2>
<p>Make your chatbot produce a "work receipt" after any important task to measure finished output, review required, time saved, and risk. This helps distinguish activity from actual value.</p>

<p>After completing a project, paste this prompt into ChatGPT, Claude, or Gemini:</p>

<p><strong>Review the work we just completed and create an AI work receipt.</strong></p>

<p><strong>Include:</strong><br>
1. Finished output: What was actually completed?<br>
2. Human baseline: How long would this likely take me manually?<br>
3. AI-assisted time: How long did this take with you?<br>
4. Review required: What did I still need to check, rewrite, or fix?<br>
5. Risk: What could be wrong, incomplete, or misleading?<br>
6. Final value estimate: Was this a small assist, a major time saver, or not worth using AI for?</p>

<p><strong>Be conservative. Do not count drafts, ideas, or unused output as completed work.</strong></p>

<p>The key is being conservative—AI excels at sounding productive, but the receipt forces it to prove the work survived real-world application. [1]</p>
